Homemade and authentic tacos de carnitas recipe

This is and authentic taco recipe made by a Mexican in Mexico, concerving all the flavor that make tacos famous.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • Cilantro
  • 1 garlic
  • Pork meat
  • Tortillas
  • Pan
  • oil
  • Pot
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

    • Steps

    1. Boil the pork for 2 hours along with half an onion, 10g of salt and a head of garlic.
    2. Take the boiled meat and cut it into small pieces, and put it to brown in a pan with oil.
    3. Cut the onion into cubes and the cilantro into strips.
    4. Heat the tortillas in a frying pan
    5. Place a tortilla on a plate and place the sliced ​​meat on top of the tortilla, then add the onion and cilantro.
